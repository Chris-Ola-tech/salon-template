Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services, booking process, and policies

1

How do I book an appointment?

To book an appointment, simply click on any "Book" button on our website. You'll be directed to our WhatsApp, where we can chat and finalize your booking instantly. It's quick, easy, and convenient!

2

Can I bring my own products for hair or nails?

Yes, you're welcome to bring your own products! We understand that some clients have specific preferences or sensitivities. However, we also use premium professional-grade products that are carefully selected for quality and safety. Please let us know in advance if you plan to bring your own products so we can prepare accordingly.

3

Do I need to book an appointment in advance?

Yes, we recommend booking at least 24‚Äì48 hours ahead to secure your preferred time slot. This ensures that our stylists can dedicate their full attention to you. Walk-ins are welcome when available, but appointments guarantee your spot and minimize wait times.

4

Do you offer home services?

Yes! We offer home services for special occasions such as bridal makeup, party preparations, and group bookings. Our mobile service brings salon-quality beauty treatments to your location. Additional fees may apply based on distance and service requirements. Contact us via WhatsApp to discuss your needs and get a personalized quote.

5

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept multiple payment methods for your convenience: cash, bank transfers, and mobile money transfers. Payment is required at the time of service or as agreed during booking. For home services and special packages, we may request a deposit to secure your appointment. All payment details will be discussed when you book via WhatsApp.

6

What is your cancellation policy?

We understand that plans change! You can cancel or reschedule your appointment at least 12 hours in advance without any charges. Late cancellations or no-shows may be subject to a fee, especially for home services. Please notify us as soon as possible via WhatsApp if you need to make changes to your booking.
